Our Story

Erets Services officially opened our doors (actually we started at a desk in the basement of my house) in the spring of 2012. However, our story began a while ago. I remember enjoying mowing our 5 acre lot as a kid. Then in high school and college I gained experience working various jobs for general contractors. Our first home was a fixer upper with a separate basement apartment, paving the way for more handy-man skills and tenant management.

We decided to officially launch a business as we realized we enjoyed doing this work, and there is a need for a one-stop shop for property owners to rely on when it comes to property maintenance and improvements.
